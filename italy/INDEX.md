## Series Overview

This Italy 2022 series documents a real-time journey of:
- **Self-discovery**: Questioning purpose, motivation, and what it means to challenge oneself
- **Cultural immersion**: Learning Italian, adapting to Italian work culture, and navigating Roman bureaucracy
- **Personal growth**: Managing struggles, building resilience, and finding joy in unexpected moments
- **Authentic storytelling**: Raw, unfiltered accounts of travel without the Instagram filter

All posts are written in Czech with a mix of humorous and reflective observations about bartending in Rome.
